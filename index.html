WHY TO CHOOSE US

KALAKAD FIBRE ARTISANS CLUSTERAIMS AT THE HOLISTIC DEVELOPMENT OF NATURAL FIBRE ARTISANS ENGAGED IN SISAL, BANANA AND PALM LEAF AND OUR PRIMARY MISSION ARE TO REVIVE THE LIVELIHOOD OF TRADITIONAL ARTISANS, TO ENCOURAGE THE WOMEN EMPOWERMENT,TO HELP IN MARKETING,AND TO REPLACE PLASTIC WITH ECO- FRIENDLY PRODUCTS.

Kalakad Fibre Artisans Cluster is a one of the Flag-ship programsunder KVIC-KRDP Scheme of Ministry of MSME, Govt. of India and is being implemented in Padmaneri village in Kalakad area, Tirunelveli Dist. This cluster Development project aims at the holistic development of natural fibre artisans engaged in sisal, banana and palm leaf in kalakad area through skill and design development, technical, technological and market interventions for their long-term sustainability and economy of scale. Further, the project focus on revitalization of traditional industries and also envisages promotion of rural enterprises / entrepreneurs.
